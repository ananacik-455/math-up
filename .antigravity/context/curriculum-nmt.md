# Curriculum & NMT Practice Mode

## Topic Library (Grades 4-11)
The structured topic tree is aligned to the Ukrainian school curriculum. The MVP covers the most NMT-tested areas:
- Arithmetic
- Fractions & percentages
- Algebra
- Geometry basics
- Equations

## NMT Practice Mode
A dedicated mode with real NMT-style questions to prepare Ukrainian students for the national exam.

**Strict Rules for NMT Mode:**
- **Format:** Multiple choice only (NMT question format).
- **Timing:** Timed sessions.
- **Assistance:** No hints allowed.
- **Coverage:** Includes a topic coverage map.
